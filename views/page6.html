About Us

We are a website whose main purpose is help in finding
a bike shop that is close, suitable and satisfying for everyone.
As bicycle enthusiasts, we are well aware of the problems involved
in searching for bicycle shops, lack of sufficient information about
opening hours, and especially the inability to find a shop that has
the right model for us. For all of these, we created Bake Stop,
a free website that aims to get you start rolling!
